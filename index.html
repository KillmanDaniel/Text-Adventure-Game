Silent Caller


     You just got to work after a patrol around the block. The donut you were eating was melting it's buttery oils around your lips until... RING RING RING! You jumped into the air. You luckily landed back into your seat. You could of easily biffed it. Somehow, you were able to answer the call in time. The call was silent. You said hello many times but you knew what was up. It was a silent caller. This is your first silent call since the academy, and those were just drills. Now it's the real deal, and you're on your own.

You track the call back and head to it's location. It led you up to a lone house in the woods.

This just isn't your day.





Where am I?
